Description

Pasteis (the plural of pastel in Portuguese) are light, thinly rolled pastry dough pockets
filled with a variety of ingredients, but most notably with cheese (queijo) and/or beef.
This version is the simple, but flavorful ground beef filling with just a few perfect seasonings.

Ingredients

  • All-purpose flour
  • Warm Water
  • Oil : any neutral flavored vegetable oil works
  • White vinegar
  • Cacha√ßa
  • Salt: add as much as you like
  • Cheese: Traditionally Mozzarella cheese is used

Steps

  1. Mix all ingredients together until a dough forms. Knead for a couple minutes until smooth.

  2. Roll it out using a rolling pin or a pasta machine.

  3. Fill with your favorite filling, fold and seal the edges.

  4. Deep fry until golden and bubbly.
